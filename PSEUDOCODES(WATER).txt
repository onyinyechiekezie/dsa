PSEUDOCODES(WATER)
Stage1
1.Prompt and collect amount of water in kilograms and initial and final temperature for the water using
	Q=M*(final temperature-initial temperature)*4184
M=weight of water in kilograms
Temperature in degree celsius.
Energy Q in joules.

Stage2
1.Prompt the user to enter amount of water in kilograms
2.Collect it.
3.Save as kilograms
4.Prompt the user to enter initial temperature of water
5.Collect it 
6.Save as initial temperature
7.Prompt the user to enter final temperature of water
8.Collect it.
9.Save as final temperature of water
10.Calculate
	kilograms*(final temperature-initial temperature)*4184
11.Collect result
12.Save as energy.
13.Print energy.

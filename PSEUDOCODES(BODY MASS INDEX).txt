PSEUDOCODES(BODY MASS INDEX)
Stage1
1.Prompt the user to enter weight in pounds
2.Collect it.
3.Save as pounds
4.Prompt the user to enter height in inches
5.Collect it.
6.Save as inches
7.Calculate
	pounds*0.45359237
8.Collect result
9.Save as Kilograms
10.Calculate 
	inches*0.0254
11.Calculate it.
12.Save as meters
13.Calculate
	kilograms / (meters*meters)
14.Collect result
15.Save as bodyMassIndex
16.Display bodyMassIndex.
